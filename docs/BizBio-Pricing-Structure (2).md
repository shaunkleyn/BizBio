## 1. Pricing Philosophy

### Value-Based Pricing
Our pricing is designed around the value we deliver to specific market segments, not arbitrary feature counts. Each vertical solves specific pain points:

- **Professional:** Replaces physical business cards and provides always-updated contact information
- **Menu:** Eliminates printing costs and enables instant menu updates
- **Retail:** Provides product showcase without full e-commerce complexity

### Transparent Pricing
- No hidden fees
- Cancel anytime
- Monthly or annual billing (save 15% with annual)
- 14-day free trial on all paid plans

### South African Focus
- Prices in ZAR (South African Rand)
- Local payment methods (PayFast)

## 9. FAQ

### 9.1 General Questions

**Q: Can I switch plans anytime?**  
A: Yes! Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: We accept all major credit/debit cards, EFT, and instant EFT through PayFast (South African payment gateway).

**Q: Is VAT included in the prices?**  
A: Yes, all prices displayed include 15% VAT as required by South African law.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
