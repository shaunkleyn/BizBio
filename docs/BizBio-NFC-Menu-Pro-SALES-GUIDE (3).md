## 5. Objection Handling

### 5.1 Price Objections

**Objection:** "R149/month is too expensive."

**Response:**
> "Let me put it in perspective. That's R4.96 per day. Less than one coffee. How much do you spend when you reprint menus? R200-R300? How often? 4-6 times per year? That's R800-R1,800 on printing alone. Menu Pro is R1,788/year AND gives you Event Mode, table personalities, analytics. You're actually saving money."

**Alternative Close:**
> "What if I showed you you'll save R20,000 this year? Would R149/month be worth it then?"

---

**Objection:** "The NFC tags are expensive."

**Response:**
> "They're a one-time purchase. R750 for 25 tags = R30 per table. Your printed menus cost R200 to reprint. After 4 reprints (one year), you've spent R800 per table. NFC tags last 3-5 years with zero reprinting. Over 3 years, you save R2,370 per table. For 25 tables, that's R59,250 saved."

**Show Calculator:**
```
Printed Menus (3 years):
R200 × 12 reprints × 25 tables = R60,000

NFC Tags (3 years):
R750 (one-time) + R5,364 (software) = R6,114

Savings: R53,886 over 3 years
```

---

### 5.2 Technical Objections

**Objection:** "My customers don't have smartphones."

**Response:**
> "In South Africa, 91% of adults have smartphones. And that number is higher for restaurant diners—closer to 95-98%. We've deployed this in 50+ restaurants. Less than 1% of customers don't have NFC phones. For them, we also provide QR codes as backup. You're covered."

**Backup Data:**
- SA smartphone penetration: 91%
- Dining-out demographic: 95%+
- NFC-enabled phones: 90%

---

**Objection:** "NFC is too complicated."

**Response:**
> "It's literally tap-and-go. Like contactless payment. Your customers already do it 10 times a day. There's no app to download, no sign-up, no passwords. Just tap table, menu appears. It's actually easier than scanning a QR code."

**Demo:**
- Show them on your phone
- Let them try it
- Instant credibility

---

**Objection:** "What if the tags break?"

**Response:**
> "NFC tags have a 3-5 year warranty. They're waterproof, spill-resistant, and durable. In 12 months of testing, we've had a 0.2% failure rate. That's 1 tag out of 500. If one breaks, we replace it free under warranty. Compare that to printed menus—how many times have they been damaged, stained, or needed replacing?"

---

**Objection:** "I don't need table personalities."

**Response:**
> "Fair enough. The good news is Menu Pro includes both Event Mode AND table personalities. Use what you need. Some customers only use Event Mode. Others go all-in on table stories. You paid for both, use whichever helps your business. Most customers start with Event Mode, then add table stories later when they see the impact."

## 10. ROI Calculators

### 10.1 Simple ROI Calculator

**Questions:**
1. How many tables? [X]
2. How often do you reprint menus per year? [Y]
3. What's the cost per reprint? [R Z]

**Calculation:**
```
Current Annual Cost = X × Y × Z

BizBio Year 1 Cost:
- NFC Tags: X × R30 = R[A]
- Menu Pro: R1,788
- Total: R[A + 1,788]

Annual Savings = [Current Cost] - [BizBio Cost]

ROI = ([Savings] / [BizBio Cost]) × 100
```

**Example:**
```
30 tables × 4 reprints × R250 = R30,000 current cost

BizBio:
- 30 tags × R30 = R900
- Software: R1,788
- Total: R2,688

Savings: R30,000 - R2,688 = R27,312

ROI: 1,016%
```
