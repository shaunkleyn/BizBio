## Pricing Philosophy

### Simple, Honest Pricing
- No hidden fees
- Cancel anytime (no contracts)
- 14-day free trial on all paid plans
- Prices exclude VAT (not VAT registered)
- Monthly billing only (keep it simple)

### Value-Based Pricing
Each plan is designed to solve specific problems:
- **Professional:** Replace physical business cards
- **Menu:** Eliminate menu printing costs
- **Retail:** Showcase products beautifully

## 8. Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Can I switch plans anytime?**  
A: Yes! Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: We accept all major credit/debit cards and EFT through PayFast.

**Q: Are prices excluding VAT?**  
A: Yes, all prices exclude VAT. We are not currently VAT registered.

**Q: Can I try before I buy?**  
A: Yes! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my plan limits?**  
A: You'll receive a notification when approaching your limit. You can upgrade or purchase add-ons to increase limits.

**Q: Can I have both Professional and Menu plans?**  
A: Yes! We offer bundle packages that combine plans at a 20% discount.

---

### Technical

**Q: Do I need technical skills?**  
A: Not at all! BizBio is designed for non-technical users. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use BizBio.

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes! We use bank-grade encryption (SSL/TLS), regular backups, and comply with POPIA (South African privacy law).

**Q: Can I export my data?**  
A: Yes, you can export your data at any time in CSV format.

**Q: What devices does BizBio work on?**  
A: All devices! BizBio works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Everything is mobile-optimized automatically.

---

### Support

**Q: What support do you offer?**  
A: All plans include email support. Response time is typically within 24-48 hours.

**Q: Do you offer training?**  
A: We provide video tutorials and documentation. Professional design services are available for hire.

---

### Billing

**Q: How does billing work?**  
A: You'll be billed monthly on the date you subscribed. Invoices are automatically emailed after each payment.

**Q: What happens when my trial ends?**  
A: You'll be reminded 3 days before trial ends. If you don't add payment info, your account moves to the Free plan (Professional) or is paused (Menu/Retail).

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**  
A: Yes, you can pause your subscription for up to 3 months. Your data is retained, but profiles become private during the pause.
