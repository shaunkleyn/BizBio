Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards and EFT through PayFast.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

Do I need technical skills?

Not at all! BizBio is designed for non-technical users. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use BizBio.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use bank-grade encryption (SSL/TLS), regular backups, and comply with POPIA (South African privacy law).
