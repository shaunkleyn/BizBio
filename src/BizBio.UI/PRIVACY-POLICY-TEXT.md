# Privacy Policy - Cookie & Local Storage Usage

**Last Updated: December 28, 2024**

## Introduction

BizBio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store data through cookies and browser local storage.

## What Data We Collect

### 1. Strictly Necessary Data (Functional)

We store the following data in your browser's local storage to provide essential functionality:

- **Subscription Plan Selection**: Your chosen menu plan (Starter, Professional, or Premium)
- **Trial Period Information**: The start date of your 14-day free trial
- **Authentication Tokens**: Session tokens to keep you logged in
- **User Preferences**: Application settings and configurations

**Legal Basis**: Legitimate interest - this data is essential for the service to function.

**Retention**: Stored until you clear your browser data or revoke consent.

### 2. Analytics & Performance Data (Optional)

With your consent, we may collect anonymous usage data:

- **Device Information**: Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- **Browser Information**: Browser type and version
- **Usage Metrics**: Page views, session duration, feature usage
- **General Location**: Country and city (IP-based, no precise GPS)
- **Performance Data**: Page load times, error rates

**Legal Basis**: Consent - you can opt out at any time.

**Purpose**: To improve our service, understand user behavior, and optimize performance.

**Retention**: Stored for up to 12 months, then anonymized or deleted.

## How We Use This Data

### Functional Data
- Manage your subscription and trial period
- Maintain your logged-in session
- Remember your preferences
- Provide personalized features

### Analytics Data
- Understand how users interact with our platform
- Identify and fix technical issues
- Improve user experience
- Plan new features based on usage patterns

## Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored **locally in your browser** (not on our servers)
- No data is shared with third parties without your explicit consent
- You have full control to delete this data at any time

## Your Rights

Under GDPR, POPIA, and CCPA, you have the right to:

1. **Access**: View what data is stored about you
2. **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
3. **Erasure**: Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
4. **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data
5. **Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
6. **Object**: Opt out of analytics and optional data collection
7. **Withdraw Consent**: Change your preferences at any time

### How to Exercise Your Rights

- **View/Delete Data**: Clear your browser's local storage
- **Change Preferences**: Click the cookie settings link in the footer
- **Request Data**: Contact us at privacy@bizbio.com

## Cookie Categories Explained

### Strictly Necessary Cookies
**Cannot be disabled** - Required for the website to function.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| userPlanId | Stores selected subscription plan | Until cleared |
| trialStartDate | Tracks trial period | Until cleared |
| auth_token | Authentication session | 30 days |
| bizbio_cookie_consent | Stores your consent preferences | 12 months |

### Analytics Cookies (Optional)
**Can be disabled** - Helps improve our service.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| _ga | Google Analytics user tracking | 2 years |
| _gid | Google Analytics session tracking | 24 hours |
| analytics_* | Custom usage tracking | 12 months |
