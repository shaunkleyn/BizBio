## 1. Connect - Digital Business Cards

> **Formerly:** BizBio Professional

### 1.1 Free Plan

**Price:** R0/month (Forever Free)

**Includes:**
- ✅ 1 digital profile
- ✅ Basic profile (choose from 5 templates)
- ✅ QR code generation
- ✅ Contact information (phone, email, website)
- ✅ Social media links (up to 5)
- ✅ vCard download
- ✅ Mobile responsive

**Limitations:**
- ❌ No analytics
- ❌ No custom branding
- ❌ No document uploads
- ❌ No profile sharing tracking
- ❌ BizBio badge on profile

### 1.2 Solo Plan ⭐ Most Popular

**Price:** R59/month

**Perfect for:** Individual professionals who need one premium profile

**Everything in Free, plus:**
- ✅ **All premium features** (see below)
- ✅ **Still just 1 profile** (perfect for solo professionals)

**Premium Features:**
- ✅ **Custom branding** (colors, fonts, logo)
- ✅ **Premium templates**
- ✅ **Remove BizBio badge**
- ✅ **Analytics dashboard** (views, clicks, geography, devices)
- ✅ **Custom profile slug** (bizbio.co.za/your-name)
- ✅ **vCard download** (save to contacts)
- ✅ **Location map** (Google Maps integration)

**Best For:**
- Solo consultants
- Freelancers
- Individual professionals
- Anyone who only needs ONE amazing profile

**Value:** All the premium features without paying for multiple profiles you don't need!
